= STARKs and SNARKs: Differences

.STARK vs SNARK
|======================================
|                | STARK    | SNARK    
| Verification   | log^2^(n)| constant 
| Proof Size     | ~400 KB  | 288 bytes
| Trusted Setup  | No       | Yes      
| Quantum Secure | Yes      | No       
|======================================
